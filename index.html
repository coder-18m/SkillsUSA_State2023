The Golden Retriever

About

The Golden Retriever has a golden yellow coat of fur, is medium size, has a broad head and short ears. Height ranges from 23-24 inches for males and 21.5-22.5 inches for females. Males weigh around 65-75 pounds, while females weigh 55-65 pounds. Life expectancy is 10-12 years.
