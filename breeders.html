Supplise

According to dogfoodadvisor.com, “Golden retriever puppies are at greater risk of developing a crippling form of hip disease… if they’re fed a diet that contains too much calcium”. With this in mind, the best and healthiest dog food for golden retrievers are:

  • Wellness Complete Health Large Breed Adult

  • Taste of the Wild Southwest Canyon

  • Non Nom Fresh


About

The Golden Retriever has a golden yellow coat of fur, is medium size, has a broad head and short ears. Height ranges from 23-24 inches for males and 21.5-22.5 inches for females. Males weigh around 65-75 pounds, while females weigh 55-65 pounds. Life expectancy is 10-12 years.
